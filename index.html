Meet Candii Flores

REALTOR® | NNA Certified Notary Signing Agent

Las Vegas isn't just where I work it's the city I've proudly called home my entire life. I've had the privilege of watching our community grow, neighborhoods evolve, and families build their futures here.

My goal isn't to convince you to buy or sell a home before you're ready. My role is to be a trusted resource someone who listens, answers your questions honestly, and guides you through the process at a pace that feels right for you.

Whether you're buying your first home, upgrading for your growing family, investing in your future, or simply exploring your options, you'll always receive honest advice, clear communication, and support that's centered around your goals not sales pressure.

Real estate is one of life's biggest decisions. I believe you deserve someone who will stand beside you, advocate for your best interests, and help you feel confident every step of the way.
